Referral Help

Clinic Referrals

How to make a referral:

The correct form to use to create a referral depends on the patient’s phase of pregnancy:

  • Antenatal patients - choose "Problem or Labour Visit" from the left nav strip, then click link to "Problem or Labour Visit."
  • Postnatal patients - choose "Postnatal/Delivery Summary" from the left nav strip, then click link to "Maternal Problem/Postnatal visit."

To create the referral, choose "Refer to UTH" from the Disposition dropdown:

If a patient is already at UTH but does not have a referral from a clinic, the UTH staff member admits the patient by following the same process, and selects "Admit to UTH" from the Disposition dropdown.

You may monitor referral status from the "Referrals" link on the left nav strip:

UTH Admissions

To admit a referred patient to UTH, click on the link under the "Reason column." This takes the user to the record from which the clinician referred the patient.

Select a value from the "Condition on Arrival" dropdown, and press submit to admit the patient. This will create an entry in the referral record:

As well as in the UTH Registry:

To discharge the patient, click the "UTH Registry" link from the left nav strip, and click the link in the "Discharge" section. Submitting the discharge form will take the user off the active referrals listing.
